Hour of Code

Hello everyone, on November 11, 2022, we(Leyla Huseynova, Zakir Ashirli, Amal Mammadzade, Nizami Guliyev) presented our code hour at secondary school No. 202 named after Ali Mustafayev. First, we informed the students about the advantages of ADA University. Later, we tried to teach children algorithms and programming principles with the help of fun games on https://code.org/. I think we taught the students something useful in this regard. We would like to express our gratitude to the entire teaching staff, led by the director of school No. 202, who made it possible for our presentation to take place. We also thank the 7b graders for their attention and interest in the lesson, and we wish them success in their future educational life.
